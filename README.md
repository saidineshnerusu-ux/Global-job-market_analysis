# Global-job-market_analysis
Global Job Market Analysis dashboard tracks openings by country, job title, month, and work environment. It reveals demand trends, highlights top roles like Data Analyst and AI Engineer, and provides insights for workforce planning, recruitment strategies, and market forecasting.
Global Job Market Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

Provides a comprehensive view of global job openings and employment trends.

Focuses on country-wise openings, job titles, monthly demand, and work environments.

Designed to support workforce planning, recruitment strategies, and market forecasting.

Key Features

Country-wise Openings: Tracks job openings from 2020–2026, showing stability with minor fluctuations.

Work Environment Distribution: Remote (34%), Hybrid (33%), Onsite (33%) — balanced demand across modes.

Monthly Trends: Highlights seasonal variations in job openings, with peaks in December and January.

Job Title Insights: Top roles include Business Analyst, Data Scientist, Data Analyst, and Machine Learning Engineer.

Filters & Customization

Industry: Retail, Technology.

Country: Singapore, UK.

Company Size: Startup.

Job Titles: Data Analyst, Data Engineer, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer.

Experience Levels: Entry, Mid, Senior.

Key Insights

Job openings remain consistent across years, averaging ~7,300 annually.

Remote, hybrid, and onsite roles are evenly distributed, reflecting flexible work trends.

Business Analyst and Data Scientist roles lead demand, each with ~8,900 openings.

Seasonal peaks in Q4 suggest year-end hiring cycles.

Recommendations

Align recruitment strategies with seasonal demand peaks.

Focus on high-demand roles like Business Analyst and Data Scientist.

Leverage balanced work environment preferences to attract diverse talent.

Expand analysis to additional countries for broader global insights.

Conclusion

The Global Job Market Analysis Dashboard consolidates critical employment data into an interactive platform. It empowers organizations to identify demand trends, optimize hiring strategies, and adapt to evolving work environments, making it a valuable tool for HR and business leaders.
